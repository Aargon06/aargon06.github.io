Welcome, I'm Aaron Molt

I am a Computer Science Master's student at Ulm University with a strong passion for software quality and engineering. Currently, I am a Research Assistant at the Institute of Software Engineering and Automotive Informatics at the TU Braunschweig. My research focuses on Quality Assurance of Configurable Systems, exploring how to ensure reliability in complex, highly customizable software environments.

Beyond my research, I am dedicated to technology education. I teach NWT (Natural Science and Technology) at Kepler Gymnasium Ulm, where I introduce students to the world of electronics and programming through hands-on Arduino projects.
