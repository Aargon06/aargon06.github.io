Researcher · Educator · Engineer

Aaron Molt

Computer Science Master's student at Ulm University and Research Assistant at the Institute of Software Engineering and Automotive Informatics at TU Braunschweig. My research focuses on Quality Assurance of Configurable Systems, exploring methods to ensure reliability in complex, highly customizable software through knowledge compilation.

Beyond research, I teach NWT at Kepler Gymnasium Ulm, introducing students to electronics and programming through hands-on Arduino projects.
